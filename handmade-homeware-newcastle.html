Croi Concrete is a homeware studio based in Newcastle upon Tyne. We make handmade concrete planters, coasters, and desk accessories — each piece individually cast and finished in the northeast of England.

Made in Newcastle, delivered UK-wide

Every piece we make is produced in our studio in Newcastle upon Tyne. We're part of a long tradition of northeast making — from the shipyards and steelworks to the glass and ceramic studios that came after. Our work is smaller in scale but the same in spirit: making things properly, in a specific place, with our hands.

We sell online through our own website and Etsy, and occasionally at local markets across Newcastle and the northeast. If you're local and want to see the work in person, get in touch.

Orders placed before 2pm Monday–Friday are typically dispatched the same day. UK delivery takes 3–5 working days with Royal Mail.

What we make

Concrete planters — indoor plant pots for succulents, cacti and houseplants. Naturally porous, heavy, and designed to last decades.

Concrete coasters — sets of four, hand-poured and sanded. A practical gift that actually gets used.

Desk accessories — pen holders, phone stands, and desk organisers in raw concrete. Minimal and functional.

Custom pieces — personalised planters, custom sizes, and gift bundles. Message us on Etsy to discuss.

FAQs — Buying from Newcastle

Can I collect in person from Newcastle?
We don't have a permanent shopfront but we do sell at local markets in Newcastle and the northeast. Message us on Etsy or email hello@croiconcrete.co.uk to ask about upcoming markets or to arrange collection.
How long does delivery take from Newcastle?
We dispatch within 1–3 working days from Newcastle. Standard UK delivery with Royal Mail takes 3–5 working days after dispatch. Express options are available at checkout.
Do you sell wholesale to Newcastle shops?
Yes, we're open to wholesale enquiries from Newcastle and northeast shops. Email hello@croiconcrete.co.uk with details of your shop and we'll send our wholesale terms.
Is Croi Concrete only available online?
Primarily yes, but we attend local markets periodically. Follow us on Instagram @croiconcrete for market announcements or sign up to our newsletter for updates.
