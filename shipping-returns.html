Returns policy

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, please contact us within 14 days of receiving your order.

  • Items must be returned in their original condition, unused and in original packaging
  • Return postage costs are the buyer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect
  • Personalised or custom items are non-returnable unless faulty
  • Refunds are processed within 5 working days of receiving the returned item

Damaged items

Concrete is robust but can occasionally be damaged in transit. If your item arrives damaged, please photograph it and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.
