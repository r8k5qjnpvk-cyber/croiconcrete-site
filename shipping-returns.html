Processing & dispatch times

Most of our concrete planters, coasters and other pieces are made to order in our Newcastle studio. Please allow 1–3 working days for your piece to be made, quality-checked, and carefully packed before dispatch. You'll receive an email notification when your order is on its way.

We pack all orders by hand using protective materials — concrete is robust, but we take no chances with transit. Every piece leaves the studio well-protected.

Returns policy

We want you to be completely happy with your Croi Concrete purchase. Every piece is individually inspected before dispatch. If something isn't right, here's what to do:

Standard returns

  • Contact us within 14 days of receiving your order at hello@croiconcrete.co.uk
  • Items must be returned unused, in their original condition and packaging
  • Return postage costs are the buyer's responsibility for standard returns
  • Refunds are processed within 5 working days of us receiving the returned item
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable on standard returns

Personalised & custom items

Personalised concrete pieces — items with names, dates, or custom inscriptions — are non-returnable unless they arrive faulty or damaged. Please check all personalisation details carefully before placing your order. Contact us before ordering if you're unsure about anything.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged or we've sent the wrong item, please photograph the damage and contact us at hello@croiconcrete.co.uk within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund including return postage, at no cost to you. We take transit damage seriously and use your feedback to improve our packing.

Packaging & sustainability

All Croi Concrete orders are packed using recycled cardboard, kraft paper, and biodegradable void fill. We don't use single-use plastic in our packaging. Outer boxes are fully recyclable. Our aim is to get your piece to you safely without unnecessary waste.

Frequently asked questions

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Contact us as soon as possible at hello@croiconcrete.co.uk. If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, we can usually update the address. Once dispatched, we're unable to redirect parcels.
Do you deliver to PO boxes?
Royal Mail can deliver to PO boxes for standard and tracked services. Please include any special instructions in the order notes at checkout.
What happens if I'm not in when my order is delivered?
Royal Mail will leave a "Something for you" card with instructions for redelivery or collection from your local delivery office. Tracked orders can be managed via the Royal Mail app.
Can I collect my order from your Newcastle studio?
We don't have a walk-in shop, but local collection from Newcastle can sometimes be arranged. Email hello@croiconcrete.co.uk and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
