Why concrete coasters?

Concrete coasters have a weight and solidity that wood, cork, or silicone alternatives can't match. When you put a cup down on a concrete coaster, it stays there. The mass is satisfying. The look is minimal and modern.

Our coasters are finished with a smooth surface that's easy to clean with a damp cloth. They don't absorb stains easily and they don't warp or degrade over time. A set of concrete coasters bought today should still be on your coffee table in twenty years.

Great as a gift

Concrete coasters are one of our bestselling gift items. They're practical — everyone uses coasters — but they're distinctive in a way that most practical gifts aren't. A set of four handmade concrete coasters says you thought about it.

Concrete Coaster FAQs

Are concrete coasters absorbent?
Unsealed concrete is naturally porous and will absorb small amounts of moisture. Our coasters are sealed to reduce this, but we recommend wiping them after use rather than leaving wet glasses on them for extended periods.
Do they scratch furniture?
We sand the undersides of our coasters to a smooth finish and apply felt pads to prevent scratching. If a pad ever comes off, self-adhesive felt pads are available from any hardware store.
How do I clean concrete coasters?
Wipe with a damp cloth and mild washing-up liquid. Don't submerge in water. Don't put in the dishwasher. Simple maintenance keeps them looking great indefinitely.
Do you sell coasters individually or as sets?
We sell both. Individual coasters and sets of four are both available. Check our shop for current stock. Sets are a better value and look better on a table.
