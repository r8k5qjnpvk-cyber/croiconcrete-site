Gift Guide: Concrete Homeware Essentials for UK Housewarmings

12 May 2026  ·  Croi Concrete Journal  ·  All posts

A good housewarming gift should earn its place in the home. It should be useful, easy to style, and personal enough to feel chosen rather than grabbed from a supermarket shelf.

Start with the room they will use most

For a living room, a set of concrete coasters is the safe bet: practical, tactile and visible every day. For a kitchen windowsill or home office, a small concrete planter with a succulent feels more personal without being fussy.

Choose smaller pieces for easier gifting

Croi pieces are deliberately compact. Small pots, coaster sets and desk accessories are easy to wrap, easy to post and easy for the recipient to place, even in a rental or a first flat.

Add meaning without over-personalising

A handmade concrete piece already carries a sense of intention because every pour has slight differences in tone and texture. If you want to make the gift more personal, pair a planter with a plant cutting or add a handwritten note.

The best Croi housewarming picks

  • A small concrete planter for succulents, cacti or herbs.
  • A set of concrete coasters for a coffee table, bedside table or home bar.
  • A concrete desk organiser for anyone setting up a new workspace.
  • A gift bundle with a planter and coaster set when you want something more substantial.

The goal is not to buy the biggest thing. It is to choose a small object that becomes part of the recipient's daily routine.
