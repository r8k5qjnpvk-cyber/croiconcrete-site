5 Unique Concrete Gifts for People Who Have Everything

18 February 2026  ·  Croi Concrete Journal  ·  All posts

We've all been there — standing in a shop at Christmas or before a birthday, surrounded by the same candles, the same bath sets, the same gift cards. If you want to give something genuinely different, handmade concrete homeware is a strong choice. Here's why, and what to go for.

1. A Personalised Concrete Planter

Our custom concrete planters can be personalised with names, dates, or short phrases. They're ideal for house moves, new parents, or anyone who loves plants. Unlike a bunch of flowers, they last forever. Pair one with a small succulent and you have a complete gift.

2. Concrete Coaster Set

Our concrete coaster sets are one of our bestsellers as gifts. Four matching coasters in a raw concrete finish — they look brilliant on a coffee table and actually get used daily. Practical gifts are underrated.

3. Concrete Desk Organiser

For the person who works from home, a concrete desk organiser or pen holder makes the kind of gift they wouldn't buy themselves. It transforms an ordinary desk into something considered.

4. Concrete Phone Holder

Our phone stands are a modern classic. Minimal, heavy enough to be satisfying, and practical. They work on bedside tables, desks, or kitchen countertops.

5. Mixed Gift Set

Contact us directly via Etsy and we can put together a custom concrete gift set — a coaster, a small planter, and a candle holder, for example. We wrap everything in tissue and include a handwritten note.

The truth is, handmade gifts communicate something that bought gifts rarely do: that you chose something made with care, for someone worth caring about.
